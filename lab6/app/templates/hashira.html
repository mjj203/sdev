The Hashira of Demon Slayer

The Hashira, also known as the Pillars, are the most powerful swordsmen of the Demon Slayer Corps. As elite members, they are pivotal in the battle against demons and play a crucial role in the storyline of "Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba".

Role and Abilities

Hashira are distinguished by their incredible strength, skill, and unique breathing techniques. Each Hashira has mastered a specific form of breathing technique, which grants them abilities far beyond that of a typical Demon Slayer.

Significance in the Story

In the series, the Hashira are not only formidable warriors but also carry significant emotional and narrative weight. They mentor younger slayers, uphold the corps' values, and face some of the most formidable demons.

Ranking of the Hashira

The Hashira, being the elite members of the Demon Slayer Corps, have varying levels of strength and skill. Here is a list ranking the Hashira from strongest to least, based on their abilities and contributions:

  1. Gyomei Himejima: The Stone Hashira
  2. Shinazugawa Sanemi: The Wind Hashira
  3. Giyu Tomioka: The Water Hashira
  4. Muichiro Tokito: The Mist Hashira
  5. Kyojuro Rengoku: The Flame Hashira
  6. Obanai Iguro: The Serpent Hashira
  7. Mitsuri Kanroji: The Love Hashira
  8. Tengen Uzui: The Sound Hashira
  9. Shinobu Kocho: The Insect Hashira
